DGPS Survey Services

We provide high-precision land surveying services using Total Station and Differential GPS (DGPS) equipment to ensure accurate positioning and reliable ground control.

Our Survey Services

  • Topographic & Boundary Survey — Accurate mapping of land features, elevations, and legal boundaries to support planning, design, and regulatory compliance.
  • Layout Marking — Precise marking of building layouts, road alignments, plot divisions, and project footprints directly on the ground.
  • Construction Set-Out — Reliable construction staking services to translate engineering drawings into accurate on-site execution.
  • Subdivision Survey — Professional land subdivision services including plotting, marking, and documentation for residential, commercial, and industrial developments.
  • Solar Pile Marking — High-accuracy pile and structure positioning for solar power projects, ensuring optimal alignment and installation efficiency.

Benefits

  • Provides high positional accuracy for infrastructure and cadastral projects.
  • Enhances survey efficiency over large or remote areas.
  • Enables real-time corrections for consistent and verifiable results.
