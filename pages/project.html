CAD Drafting
Technical Drawings with Precision and Clarity

At Cartospatial Technologies, our CAD Drafting services convert raw survey data, sketches, and concepts into accurate and detailed technical drawings. Whether it's 2D drafting or 3D modeling, we ensure clean, standards-compliant outputs that support engineering, architectural, and infrastructure development workflows.

Key Features of Our CAD Drafting Services

2D Drafting & Detailing

  • Preparation of floor plans, elevations, sections, and site layouts.
  • Topographic maps, contour plans, and utility layouts.

3D Modeling & Visualization

  • 3D terrain models, structures, and infrastructure models.
  • Massing studies and visualization-ready outputs.

Survey to CAD Conversion

  • Clean conversion of Total Station, DGPS, and LiDAR data to DWG/DXF.
  • Layered drawings for terrain, boundaries, utilities, and built structures.
LiDAR Survey
High-Density 3D Mapping with Laser Precision

Cartospatial Technologies offers LiDAR Surveying services that provide incredibly accurate and high-density 3D data. Using airborne, terrestrial, and mobile LiDAR systems, we capture millions of laser points per second to create detailed topographic maps, terrain models, and feature-rich 3D representations of any environment—natural or built.

Key Features of Our LiDAR Surveys

High-Density Point Clouds

  • Up to several million points per second.
  • Ultra-dense 3D data for micro-level terrain features.

Multi-Platform Deployment

  • Airborne LiDAR (UAV, helicopter, or aircraft).
  • Terrestrial LiDAR for buildings, structures, and landscapes.
  • Mobile LiDAR for road and railway corridors.
DGPS Survey
High-Precision Geospatial Positioning with Differential GPS

At Cartospatial Technologies, our DGPS (Differential Global Positioning System) Surveys are designed for centimeter-level positioning accuracy across diverse terrains. Using state-of-the-art dual-frequency GNSS receivers, we deliver accurate, reliable, and georeferenced data critical for infrastructure planning, land development, and asset mapping.

Key Features of Our DGPS Surveys

High Accuracy Positioning

  • Achieves sub-meter to centimeter-level accuracy.
  • RTK and PPK solutions available based on site requirements.

Dual-Frequency GNSS Receivers

  • Supports GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou constellations.
  • Enhanced satellite connectivity and signal stability in tough environments.
